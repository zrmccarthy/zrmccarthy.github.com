How to be a Man Calendar


One thing I try very hard to do in my work is to maintain a sense of humor and lightness, because otherwise I would go crazy. Most of the time, the work has no room for this humor but occasionally I get a project where I can let this out. The assignment was to create a "How-To" calendar, and I chose to portray a (somewhat) legitimate subject in a satirical way. While the pressure put on women in our society to be a certain way is absolutely a much more prominent problem, it is definitely there for men as well. I find the Ford-esque commercials of giant, bearded men in flannel grilling meat in their huge trucks hilarious and depressing, and was my main source of inspiration for this calendar.
