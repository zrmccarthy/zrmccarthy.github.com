Cradle to the Grave


"From the Cradle to the Grave" is a term that refers to the lifespan of any object, from its creation to it being disposed of, and what happens after this disposal. This encompasses the materials used to make it, its environmental effects, how to correctly and safely dispose of it, and much more. A two-part project, this was very challenging due to the amount of content to pack into this, as well as the time frame we had to complete this. I chose air fresheners as my object, and focused on how terrible for the environment and your health they are (which I had not known). I went for a light, cheery, and bright feel to reflect the incredibly cheesy air freshener commercials. The final hardcover book was done from scratch.
