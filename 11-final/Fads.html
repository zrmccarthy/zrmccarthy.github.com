Fads Infograph & Animation


Being my first experience in Information Architecture, this project was a big learning experience. The 1st Part, an infograph, started as collecting 100 pieces of data of a personal "collection" and sifting through them meticulously. We then needed to organize them logically, create various categories of metadata, and finally create a storyline or theme to portray through the infograph. Fads were a huge (and slightly embarrassing) part of my life, or any kid's life for that matter. Frosted tips, yo-yos, Nintendo, the XFL...you name it, I probably was into it at some point. I thought deep back into the childhood memories to dig up 100 fads (and called my mom quite a few times in the process). A very nostalgic project.



The next part of the assignment was to focus in on one aspect of our infograph, and create an accompanying animation. I chose to focus in on the idea of materiality, and how it can be a negative thing in too heavy doses. See the animation below:
