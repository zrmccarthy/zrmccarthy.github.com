John Cage Book


John Cage, Love him or hate him, was a HUGE inspiration and innovator for what is considered music, and for the future of music in general. For this project, we were to create an in-depth book about Mr. Cage and a certain aspect of his life and/or work through the use of carious articles and texts. I focused on his rise to fame as a conductor, and his choice to use this fame to go and do whatever the hell he wanted, rather than what was going to make him money. The final hardcover book was made from scratch.
