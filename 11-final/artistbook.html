Out of the Box Artist Book


Being my first experience in bookmaking, this project was a blast. The objective was to first write an essay connecting two artists from a provided list. The two I chose were Diane Arbus and Chuck Close. Both of these talented artists started off their careers in a certain niche that they grew comfortable being in. They didn't start becoming known worldwide until they chose to break out of that niche, and try something new and interesting. This led to the title of my book, "Out of the Box", and also led to the theme of laying out the book in a very untraditional way. This was the first hardcover book I made from scratch, and was a great learning experience after messing it up multiple times.
