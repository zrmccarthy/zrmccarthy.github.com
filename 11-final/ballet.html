How to Piourette


A very interesting project for Information Architecture, it was meant to be one of our first experiences working as a group. In small groups, we were to find a talent of one person in the group that the others didn't share. This person was to teach the others how to perform the task, and we photographed and documented the whole process. Using that information, we were to simplify the motions of the action into a clear and precise diagram meant for someone completely new to the action. Our group worked with the ballet move of pirouetting, and as you can imagine, me and a fellow male learning this move was equally hilarious, saddening, and successful in making our final diagram clear.
