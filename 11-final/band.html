Thrice Poster


One of my first projects done at Mass Art, this one is still a personal favorite of mine. The point of the project was to simply design a poster for a specific song, by a band of our choice. Thrice is a longtime favorite band of mine, and I chose to design for the song "Digital Sea". The following line was my inspiration:

"And my voice goes, to ones and zeroes."
