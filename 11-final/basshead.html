Oenamatopeia


My first introduction to the massively intimidating and awesome program known as After Effects, this was a grueling but rewarding project. After choosing an instrumental song of our choice, we were to visually graph the layout of the song through words and type as sounds. Once this type score was completed, we translated it into a 1 minute kinetic type animation. The song I use is Basshead by Bassnectar!
