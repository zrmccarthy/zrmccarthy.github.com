Ireland Travel Poster


After tediously redesigning an extremely poor train schedule, I got to the enjoyable part of designing a travel poster for a vacation route of my choice. Being Irish, I immediately was drawn to the Ireland circuit. With so many options to come off as incredibly cliche with this poster, I did everything in my power not to be. I wanted to focus on the beautiful old-style churches and stained glass that you can visit while there. The amount of color in these churches is tremendous and I made sure to reflect that in my poster.
