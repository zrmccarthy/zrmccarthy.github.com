Welcome Home Book


One of my favorite classes at Mass Art has been Print Production. The class is essentially a semester-long freeelance project, done as a class. It taught me so much about printing (obviously), getting quotes, communicating with a printing rep, working as a group and assigning certain tasks to individuals with those strengths, and bringing all these elements into the creation of a final product. This book was designed for new housing sections being built for low-income families, and this book was to act as a guide to get used to their new home. Many families were Spanish-speaking, so therefore the books reads through in English until it is flipped over, where it can be read in Spanish. Overall, this was a fantastic real-world learning experience!
